About Us

TravelCo

We are a booking company in Botswana, specializing in creating and organizing African safari and cultural experiences

At TravelCo, we don't just plan and organize your stay - we awaken your sense of wonder. Based in Maun, Botswana, we specialize in handcrafed tours and safari experiences across Southern Africa's best and most iconic destinations.

Our Mission

To deliver unforgettable, responsible safari adventures that celebrate Southern Africa's wild beauty while empowering local communities.
